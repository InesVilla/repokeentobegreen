What is a Clean Energy Council Approved Retailer?

The Clean Energy Council (CEC for short) is the peak body for the clean energy industry in Australia and represents and works with hundreds of leading businesses operating in solar, wind, energy efficiency, hydro, bioenergy, energy storage, geothermal, and marine along with more than 5000 solar installers. They developed the Code of Conduct to provide consumers peace of mind that they have made the right choice when deciding on which company to purchase a solar power system from by selecting a retailer that is committed to superior quality and service. The Code of Conduct was also created to weed out a lot of the unscrupulous solar companies and installers that have plagued the industry over the years with poor products, workmanship, and customer service and support.

Why choose an Approved Solar Retailer?

Approved Solar Retailers: have been approved by the Clean Energy Council as demonstrating their commitment to responsible sales and marketing activities, and solar industry best practice provide a five-year, whole-of-system warranty will ensure a Clean Energy Council accredited installer sets up your system The Approved Solar Retailer program is voluntary and approved by the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission. The program aims to lift the bar higher than the minimum requirements set by law and to bring about a better standard of service within the solar industry
