Commercial Solar Power

Keen 2B Green has provided a number of businesses and organizations with commercial solar power solutions across Brisbane, The Sunshine Coast, Toowoomba, The Gold Coast, and Northern News South Wales areas, across the manufacturing and business sectors and can provide commercial-sized solar energy solutions for schools, supermarkets, and sporting clubs, etc. We can help your business achieve financial and environmental goals, whilst making the investment in renewable energy a profitable and easy experience. Installing renewable energy generating capacity to offset your electricity consumption is a smart business decision in terms of insulating your business from the escalating cost of electricity. A solar power solution also demonstrates your business’s commitment to contributing to Australia’s Renewable Energy Target for the year 2020. Any business with a medium to large roof space could reap the rewards of a commercial-scale solar power system; we can install commercial solar systems from 5 kilowatts to 990 kilowatts.

Benefits

We can manage every aspect of your solar project:

  • Site analysis and system recommendation
  • System design
  • Small Scale Technology Certificate management
  • Paperwork and Approvals
  • Installation
  • System monitoring

Some of the benefits of commercial solar power include:

  • Protect your building/company against future electricity price rises.
  • Increase the value of your building by improving your NABERs/Greenstar rating.
  • Generate clean, green energy for decades.
  • Improve your company’s green credentials in the eyes of your consumers.
